"Be a part of our home."

About one-third of people around the world reported feeling stressed, worried, and/or angry in 2019. Stress, anxiety, anger, worries, depression and a lot more terrible feelings become part of our daily lives because we can’t manage both our feelings and problems at the same time. Plenty of people are trying to survive from those hunting emotions. That’s why we built HOME. But with HOME, they can rest and forget those problems for a while. HOME will provide you very special care and services freely. HOME will be your home, a place where you feel belong, a place where you can relax, a place where you can speak out what you feel and think openly and a place where you can learn to manage, fight and overcome your emotions, to love yourself and to live your life better. If you are angry, HOME will make you calm or will let you explode. If you are stressed, HOME will comfort you with relax timer, pleasant music and if you want to talk, you can tell in the chat. We believe and hope that our HOME is authentic and supportive because our aim is to offer a safe place for everyone to rest, relax and relieve.

"Be a part of our home."

Approach

HOME have 5 major services: tests for your mental health condition, mediation programs, relaxation timer, daily affirmation and chat to open freely. Our users’ favorite service is relaxation timer which let them take a full rest for a period. For now, we have 2M+ family members in HOME who improved effectively in their mental health suffering and management. 80% of the family members gave 4 out of 5 for staying in HOME. However, we are continuously processing to make HOME more convenient, more comfortable, warmer and safer.

Our future plan

Since numerous family members are requesting for more music playlist and meditation programs, we are currently working for it. We have been planning to create more meditation programs with more coaches from different countries. You will be able to Relax with more choices for modes such as city mode, village mode, home mode, library mode and fantasy mode. In 2023 December, we are going to introduce you with the healing HOME art gallery which have a calm healing effect as its name. Something which we want to announce proudly is HOME application will be produced soon. HOME is always making sure that all the family members can rest, relax and relieve comfortably in our HOME.
