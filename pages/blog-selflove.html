Self-love

noun | /ˌsɛlfˈlʌv/

  • regard for one's own well-being and happiness
      Dear me...
      Don’t neglect others for selfishness
      Don’t neglect yourself and always put others first
      Let’s go in between and focus on ourselves for Self-care>

TIPS FOR SELF CARE

  1. Don’t compare yourself with others
  2. Accept yourself and the imperfections
  3. Try to let go, if something isn’t right for you
  4. Give time for yourself
  5. Escape from the past
  6. Don’t blame yourself for the mistakes that you did
  7. Believe in yourself
  8. Let yourself get hurt enough when things are tough
  9. Do good things without hoping for something
  10. Notice the beauty around you
“A flower does not think of competing with the flower next to it. It just blooms.”
Be happy with what you have

Make joy out of the little things around you. Find your own happiness, find what makes you happy, and find what puts a smile on your face. Follow your heart. Even if you can’t find it, everybody deserves happiness.

Love yourself before you love others

When you love yourself, you don't need to make others love you. Since you have set your own standard, you will know who is treating you right or wrong.

Art of letting go

Let go of the things you need to let go of. When you hold onto the things that are toxic for you, you’re the only person who will get hurt. Yesterday is history, Tomorrow is a mystery, but Today is a gift. That is why it is called the present.

The pull of comparison

Comparisons can be good if you are just setting them as a goal. But when you start comparing yourself, you will think that you are not good enough and then start to feel that black hole in your soul and body.
